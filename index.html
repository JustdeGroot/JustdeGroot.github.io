Research Interests

My research interests focus on the statistical side. The basis in more inference focused Econometrics has led me to study Extreme Value Theory, and associated components. In particular, I am interested in the extending the Extremes literature, with a particular focus on:

  • Scale regression in the POT and BM frameworks.
  • Causality from the Rubin Causal Model point of view.
  • Extending the Hyperplane Characterization.
  • Hypersphere statistics for Extremes.

In addition, I am interested in applying extreme value theory to problems outside the field. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • High-Dimensional Statistics.
  • Error Propagation in Optimization.
  • Extreme Meteorological rainfall patterns.
  • Financial tail events.
